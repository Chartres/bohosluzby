No account or login is required — open the app and it works immediately.

Guideline 4.2 note: the app is fully functional OFFLINE. To verify, enable
Airplane Mode and open the app — the list of nearby Catholic masses still loads
(church + service data ships inside the app). The map tiles are the only part
that needs a network connection.

Location permission is optional: if denied, the app shows all churches; if
granted, it sorts them by distance. Location is used only on-device and is never
transmitted.

Data source: the official public mass registry of the Czech Bishops' Conference.
